Welcome. I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at NC State University.
My research primarily centers on the application of dynamic optimization methods to address environmental and resource management problems, with a particular focus on structural and observational uncertainties.

Following the academic year 2023-2024, I will be joining the STEPS Center as a Postdoctoral Fellow and will be on the job market during the 2024-2025 academic year.

Please feel free to reach me at ccho5@ncsu.edu
