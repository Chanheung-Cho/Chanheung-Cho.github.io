Welcome!
I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at NC State University. My research focuses on Resource and Environmental Economics, Bioeconomics, and Dynamic Decision Modeling, with a particular focus on the dynamic management of resources under structural and observational uncertainty. I hold a Ph.D. in Economics from North Carolina State University, Masters degrees from the University of Wisconsin-madison and Ajou University, and a BA degree from Ajou University.

In 2024 fall, I will be joining the STEPS Center as a Postdoctoral Research Scholar, and will be on the job market during the 2024-2025 academic year.
